About

Zijia Yu is a PhD candidate in the Department of Psychiatry, University of Alberta. Her research uses optical in vivo imaging, behavioral assays, and custom MATLAB/Python pipelines to study cortical hyperexcitability, sensory processing, synaptic plasticity, social and repetitive behaviour, and translational models of neurodevelopmental and psychiatric conditions.
